SCOPES OF USING CHATBOT

Customer Support

24/7 customer service. Chatbots offer customers the opportunity to get answers to their questions at any time, so they don't have to wait for a response from a voicemail or email message. The best chatbots can also offer the customer the feeling of conversing with a live operator at any time.

Product Suggestion

Chatbots can also be used to collect data about your visitors and use it to make better product suggestions and recommendations. Understanding customer inquiries, their needs and preferences can allow you to personalize product pages and build customer loyalty and affinity.

Virtual Assistant

Virtual Assistants have a wider scope and can perform a range of tasks, for example comparing products or finding the best product based on the given features. It is also deployed to tasks like decision making and e-commerce. Unlike chatbots, virtual assistants mature gradually with use.
